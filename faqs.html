Frequently Asked Questions

A plain sheet, often recognized as a cement plain sheet or board, is a construction material distinguished by its flat, unadorned surface. Comprising primarily cement, it serves as a fundamental component in various construction and architectural applications.

A plain sheet stands as a versatile and indispensable material for both interior and exterior applications. Its multipurpose functionality makes it ideal for applications such as partitioning, creating false ceilings, and constructing mezzanines, contributing to the aesthetic and structural elements of a space.

Certainly, there is a selection of standard thicknesses to accommodate diverse construction needs. These options range from 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 12mm, 14mm, 16mm, 18mm, 20mm, providing flexibility for different projects and structural requirements.

The availability of standard sizes, including 6x2, 6x2.5, 6x3, 7x3, and 8x4, caters to a variety of construction dimensions. Additionally, customization based on specific demands ensures that the material aligns perfectly with the unique requirements of a project.

Yes, these cement plain sheets boast exceptional weather resistance, making them impervious to the effects of varying climatic conditions. Furthermore, they exhibit resistance to termites, swelling, and are fire-retardant, ensuring durability and safety in diverse environments.

Absolutely, the utility of these sheets extends to hill site constructions, finding prevalent use in the development of resorts, hotels, bungalows, and farmhouses. Their structural integrity and weather-resistant properties make them an excellent choice for elevated terrains.

Indeed, cement plain sheets are extensively employed in industrial settings, proving versatile across various industries. Particularly valuable in chemical, oil, water, and fire-related industries, these sheets demonstrate resilience in demanding environments.

Yes, the adaptability of cement plain sheets allows for the construction of entire bungalows or rooms. Their availability in different thicknesses enables tailored usage, ensuring structural stability and aesthetic appeal in comprehensive construction projects.

Approximately 70% of the material can be effectively repurposed for use on other construction sites, attesting to its sustainability and cost-efficiency in the realm of building materials.

Following installation, these sheets offer a canvas for creative expression. Decoration options include the application of paint, polish, decorative paints, or texture paints. Additionally, they can be adorned with lamination, wallpapers, or even ceramic tiles using readily available tile adhesives from hardware stores. These options not only enhance aesthetics but also contribute to a personalized and refined finish.
