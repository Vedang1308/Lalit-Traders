Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Lalit Traders, we strive to provide a transparent and customer-friendly Cancellation & Refund Policy. Please review the following points carefully to understand our terms and conditions:

1) Zero Cancellation Charges

We understand that plans may change. Therefore, we offer zero cancellation charges until the materials are not loaded or until we have not received 100% payment. This provides you the flexibility to make adjustments without incurring any additional costs.

2) Cancellation Charges after Material Loading

In the event that the material is loaded and the vehicle is within our premises, a minimum cancellation fee of Rs.150/- will be applicable. This fee is applicable for orders with a total amount less than Rs.2000/- or up to Rs.2000/-. This policy ensures a fair and standardized approach to cancellations after the loading process has commenced.

3) Payment Refund Process

Our refund process is customer-centric. To initiate a payment refund, we require a confirmation from the customer in written format. Once we receive your cancellation request, our team will promptly process the refund to ensure a smooth and efficient transaction. Your satisfaction is our priority.
