About Us

What we do, and what we are.


Welcome to Lalit Traders

Embark on a journey with Lalit Traders, where innovation meets reliability. Established in 1995, our story is one of evolution and success. In 1997, we took a bold step into the world of cement sheets, initially uncertain but driven by a passion for quality and excellence.

Witnessing the resounding success of our cement sheets within a few short months, we didn't stop there. We expanded our horizon, introducing groundbreaking cement sheet doors that have become synonymous with Lalit Traders' commitment to excellence.


Over the last 29 years, we've stood as a symbol of service and reliability. While many cement sheet manufacturers seek our collaboration, our allegiance to the distinguished products of Sahyadri Industries remains unwavering.

Beyond being a distributor, Lalit Traders is a testament to enduring partnerships and a dedication to unparalleled service. As we reflect on our journey, join us in celebrating the milestones, relationships, and the lasting impact we've made on the cement sheet industry.

Experience Lalit Traders—where quality meets innovation, and service defines excellence. Let's continue setting standards and exceeding expectations together.
